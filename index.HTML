🏆 The Gold Standard of Winter Wheat: Engineering Ethiopia's Agricultural Future

At Selecta Ethiopia, our winter wheat production will be defined by a commitment to agricultural precision and national food security. By utilizing high-yielding, improved seed varieties specifically selected for the Ethiopian climate, we expect to achieve productivity levels that far exceed traditional benchmarks. Our upcoming harvest won't just be 'food'—it will provide a certified supply of elite seed stock that meets the most rigorous standards for genetic purity, high germination rates, and overall grain quality. Engineered through a sophisticated mixed irrigation system and expert land preparation, our wheat stands as a model for modern farming, preparing to provide local growers with the resilient, high-performance seeds necessary to transform Ethiopia's agricultural future.

Project Overview

30 Hectares Mixed Farming

22 hectares of premium Kekeba wheat + 8 hectares of Russet F1 onion. Engineered for maximum yield and sustainability.

Advanced Irrigation

Deep borehole underground water source with integrated mixed system: Gravity, Sprinkler & Drip irrigation.

Expected Output

Wheat: 42+ quintals/hectare | Onion: 20-25 tons/acre. Elite seed stock for national distribution.

Strategic Partnership

Aligned with Amhara Agriculture Bureau & North Achefer Agriculture Office. Supported by the National Winter Wheat Initiative.
